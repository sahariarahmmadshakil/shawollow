About Me

Shawna Edwards has amazed a loyal fan base eager to learn more about her haircare secrets, fashion style, beauty rituals, and her everyday life as the Executive Director of The Key Haven Families, a licensed agency that houses foster children. The Key Haven Families is a nonprofit organization that houses foster children in Central Florida. Currently licensed for 18 children and three homes used for Residential Care, Group Home Care and emergency services. In the saturated social media market, Shawna has created her own niche viewpoint, distinguishing herself amongst the many others, as a strong voice, celebrating diversity, body positivity, motherhood, style, and selfcare.
